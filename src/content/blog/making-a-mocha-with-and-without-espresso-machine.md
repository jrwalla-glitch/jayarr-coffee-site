The mocha is the perfect drink for anyone who loves both coffee and chocolate. Rich, warming, and satisfying, it's one of the most popular coffee shop orders -- and one of the easiest to recreate at home, whether or not you own an espresso machine.

## With an Espresso Machine

If you have an espresso machine, the classic approach is straightforward. Pull a double shot of espresso, add **chocolate sauce or cocoa powder** to your cup, pour the espresso over it and stir until combined, then top with steamed milk. Finish with whipped cream if you're feeling indulgent.

## Without an Espresso Machine

No espresso machine? No problem. Brew a strong cup of coffee using a **French press, AeroPress, or Moka pot** -- you want something concentrated enough to stand up to the chocolate and milk. Mix in your chocolate, add hot milk (frothed if possible using a handheld frother or a jar-and-microwave method), and you've got a mocha that rivals anything from a cafe.
