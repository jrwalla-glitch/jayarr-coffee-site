## What Makes the OXO Brew Pour Over Stand Out

The OXO Brew Pour Over is designed for simplicity. It's a clean, well-built pour-over dripper that sits on top of your mug or carafe and lets you brew a single serving of filtered coffee with minimal effort. The design is straightforward — add your filter, add your grounds, pour hot water, and let gravity do the rest.

OXO is known for thoughtful, ergonomic design across their product line, and the pour-over brewer is no exception. It's compact enough for small kitchens and easy to clean when you're done.

## The Brewing Basics

Like most pour-over methods, the key variables are your **grind size**, **water temperature**, and **pour technique**. A medium grind works well with this brewer. Use water just off the boil (around 200 degrees Fahrenheit), and pour in slow, steady circles to ensure even saturation of the grounds.

The result is a **clean, bright cup** that highlights the natural flavors of your beans — exactly what you want from a pour-over setup. If you're looking for a simple entry point into manual brewing without the precision demands of something like a V60, the OXO Brew Pour Over is a solid, affordable choice.
