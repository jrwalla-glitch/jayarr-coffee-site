## Making It at Home

A caramel brulee latte combines strong brewed coffee or espresso with steamed milk and **caramel brulee sauce** -- that deep, toasty caramel flavor that sets it apart from a regular caramel latte. The "brulee" element adds a slightly smoky, torched-sugar quality that makes this drink feel special.

## Tips for the Best Result

Use a bold, dark roast coffee or a double shot of espresso as your base -- the strong coffee flavor needs to hold its own against the sweetness of the caramel. Froth your milk well for a creamy, latte-shop texture, and don't skip the whipped cream on top if you're going for the full experience.
