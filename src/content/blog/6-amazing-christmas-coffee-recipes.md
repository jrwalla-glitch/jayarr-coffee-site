## Holiday Coffee at Home

There is something special about a well-made holiday coffee drink. The warm spices, the rich flavors, the seasonal ingredients — they transform an everyday cup into something that feels like a celebration. Check out the video above for six of our favorite Christmas coffee recipes, complete with step-by-step instructions for making each one at home.

From classic gingerbread and peppermint combinations to more creative seasonal concoctions, these recipes are designed to be approachable for any home brewer. No barista training required — just good coffee, a few seasonal ingredients, and a willingness to experiment.
