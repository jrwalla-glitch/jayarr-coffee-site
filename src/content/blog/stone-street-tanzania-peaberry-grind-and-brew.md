**Tanzania Peaberry** is one of those coffees that stands out the moment you see the beans. Unlike standard coffee cherries that produce two flat-sided beans, peaberry cherries produce just one small, round bean -- and many believe that single bean absorbs all the flavor meant for two. We picked up a bag from **Stone Street** to grind and brew it fresh.

## What Makes Peaberry Special

Peaberry beans occur naturally in about **5 to 10%** of all coffee cherries. Because only one bean develops instead of the usual two, peaberries are rounder and denser. Many coffee enthusiasts believe this concentrated growth produces a more intense, complex flavor. Tanzanian peaberry in particular is prized for its **bright acidity**, **wine-like body**, and notes of **black currant, citrus, and cocoa**.

## Stone Street Tanzania Peaberry

**Stone Street** is known for sourcing quality single origin beans, and their Tanzania Peaberry is a favorite among specialty coffee fans. The beans have a distinctive appearance -- small, round, and evenly roasted. Grinding them fresh releases a vibrant, aromatic bouquet that hints at the complexity in the cup.

## The Brew

Freshly ground and brewed, this coffee delivers a lively, bright cup with layered flavors. If you enjoy single origin coffees with character and complexity, Tanzania Peaberry is well worth exploring -- and Stone Street's offering is a solid way to try it.
