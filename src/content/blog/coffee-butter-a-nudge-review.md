What if you could skip the brewing, skip the cream and sugar, and get your morning caffeine fix by spreading it on toast? That is exactly the premise behind **Nudge Coffee Butter** -- a coffee spread that packs the equivalent of one full cup of coffee into just two tablespoons. We tried both flavors and were genuinely surprised by the results.

## What Is Coffee Butter?

**Nudge Coffee Butter** is a spreadable coffee product that looks a lot like chocolate spread but contains no chocolate whatsoever. It comes in two varieties:

- **Colombian Original** -- A medium-strength coffee (rated 3 out of 5 on their strength scale) with **40 beans per serving**
- **Italian Espresso Roast** -- A darker, richer option (rated 4 out of 5) with **26 beans per serving** due to the darker roast

Both deliver the caffeine of a full cup of coffee in every two-tablespoon serving.

## The Taste Test

**Colombian Original:** This one has a beautiful, rich flavor -- smooth and creamy with bold coffee taste. Here is the wild part: despite containing absolutely no chocolate, it genuinely tastes like a **chocolate mocha coffee**. The flavor is so good that resisting the urge to eat it straight off the knife is a real challenge. Bold, creamy, and surprisingly addictive.

**Italian Espresso Roast:** Slightly different but still delicious. It does not taste as dark as you might expect from an espresso roast -- in fact, it is not quite as bold as the Colombian. Still very enjoyable, but the Colombian takes the crown for overall flavor.

**Our pick:** The **Colombian Original** wins for its bolder, creamier taste and that uncanny mocha-like quality.

## Why This Concept Works

The appeal goes beyond novelty. Think about a rushed morning: instead of waiting 10 minutes to brew coffee, adding cream and sugar, and sipping while you get ready, you can make toast, spread on Nudge, and walk out the door with your breakfast and caffeine covered in one step.

It works on more than just toast, too. Spread it on waffles, dip strawberries or raspberries in it, add it to a bagel -- the options are wide open. For anyone who does not want the carbs from toast, just dipping fruit into a small serving gives you a quick caffeine boost with some nutrition on the side.

## The Bottom Line

**Nudge Coffee Butter** is one of the more creative coffee products we have come across, and it genuinely delivers. The flavor is rich enough to enjoy on its own, the caffeine content is real, and the convenience factor is hard to beat for busy mornings. If you are looking for a new way to get your coffee fix -- or just want to try something genuinely different -- this is worth checking out.
