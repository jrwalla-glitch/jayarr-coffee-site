Sometimes the best coffee drinks are the ones you'd never think to make. A coffee and cola ice cream float might sound unconventional, but it's a surprisingly refreshing combination that brings together the richness of coffee, the fizzy sweetness of cola, and the creamy indulgence of ice cream.

## How to Make It

This recipe could not be simpler. Start with a tall glass, add a generous scoop or two of **vanilla ice cream**, pour cold brew or chilled coffee over the top, and then add cola until the glass is full. The carbonation from the cola creates a frothy, effervescent layer as it hits the ice cream, while the coffee adds depth and complexity that a standard cola float can't match.

## Tips for the Best Float

Use a **bold cold brew concentrate** so the coffee flavor holds its own against the sweetness of the cola and ice cream. A quality vanilla ice cream works best as the neutral creaminess lets the coffee and cola shine. For an extra touch, try it with a dark roast cold brew -- the slight bitterness pairs beautifully with the sweet, fizzy cola.
