Some coffee experiments sound questionable on paper. Mixing coffee with cream soda is one of them. But sometimes the strangest pairings turn out to be surprisingly good — and this one is worth trying at least once.

## The Experiment

The idea is simple: combine brewed coffee with cream soda and see what happens. The vanilla sweetness of the cream soda pairs with the roasted bitterness of the coffee in a way that is unexpectedly harmonious. If you enjoy sweet, creamy coffee drinks, this quick and easy combination might just earn a spot in your rotation. Give it a try and let us know what you think.
